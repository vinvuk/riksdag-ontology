# Competency Questions

These questions define the minimum expressiveness the ontology must support. Each should be answerable via SPARQL over the populated knowledge graph.

## Members and Organizations

1. **Which party does MP X belong to during session Y?**
2. **Which committees does MP X serve on, and in what role?**
3. **Which MPs switched parties between sessions?**
4. **Which MPs from electoral district D are in the current session?**
5. **Who is the chair of committee C?**

## Documents

6. **What motions reference government bill P?**
7. **What committee handled proposition P, and what was the decision?**
8. **What documents has committee C produced in session S?**
9. **Which committee reports cite a specific SFS law?**
10. **Who authored motion M, and what party do they belong to?**

## Voting

11. **How did MP X vote on betänkande Z, point N?**
12. **What is the vote distribution (Ja/Nej/Avstår/Frånvarande) per party on voting event V?**
13. **Which MPs from party P voted against the party majority in vote V?**
14. **What is MP X's attendance rate across all votes in session S?**

## Legislative Process

15. **What is the full timeline for government bill P (submission → referral → committee report → debate → decision)?**
16. **Which propositions were withdrawn before reaching a chamber vote?**
17. **Which committee reports resulted in a tillkännagivande?**

## Cross-domain

18. **Which ministers have received the most interpellations in session S?**
19. **Which topics (from motions) are most frequently associated with party P?**
20. **For a given SFS statute, trace back to the original SOU, proposition, and betänkande.**
